Schedule

Till 9 PM

Registration Process

  • People scan their id cards and get into teams.
  • Each team will be assigned a number (Team 1,2,3 etc.) and a table where they are supposed to work out of.
  • Exits will not be opened at this point of time.
  • Team formation should take place before event starts.

9:00 PM - 9:30 PM

Inauguration Process

  • Inauguration ceremony with candle lighting by a distinguished guest.
  • The basic guidelines of Competition will be laid down at this point.

09:30 PM - 12:00 AM

Guest Lecture

  • Insightful lecture providing foundational ideas relevant to the competition.
  • Refreshments will be served towards the end of the lecture.

12:00 AM - 08:00 AM

Project Development

  • Presentation of the hackathon theme and project focus areas.
  • Team brainstorming session for project ideas.
  • Consultation with panelists and technical mentors.
  • Brainstorming, project planning, and initial development.
  • Relaxation and breakfast break after 08:00 AM.

09:00 AM - 11:00 AM

IEEE ComSoc Speaker Session

  • Engaging talk by an esteemed IEEE ComSoc representative.

11:00 AM - 01:00 PM

Idea Review

  • Team presentations to judges with Q/A.
  • Preliminary elimination round.

01:00 PM - 02:00 PM

Lunch Break

  • Refuel and socialize over lunch. It's also a great time to reflect on feedback and prepare for the next phase.

05:00 PM - 06:00 PM

Review 1

  • Judges and mentors will collaborate closely with each team to bolster areas that need enhancement.
  • The presence of the technical team is crucial for evaluating and providing scores for each team's efforts.
  • Teams will be granted specific periods to practice their presentations and polish their project pitches.
  • Expert advice on narrative crafting, public speaking, and demonstration strategies will be available to all teams.
  • Assistance will be offered to identify improvement opportunities, helping teams to further refine and develop their projects.

11:00 PM - 12:00 AM

Review 2

  • Continuation of project review and refinement.
  • Light snacks and refreshments will be provided.

06:00 AM onwards

Final Presentation and Judging

  • Submit your final projects by the set deadline.
  • Ensure your project complies with the provided guidelines and criteria.
  • Present your project to our panel of judges and await their evaluation.
  • Take your turn to impress the judges and audience with your project during your allocated presentation time.

TBD

Closing Ceremony

  • Join us as we celebrate the end of an exhilarating journey.
  • Recap of the event's highlights.
  • Announcement of post-event activities and opportunities for continued collaboration and networking.
