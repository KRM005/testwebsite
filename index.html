What is Hack-a-Tron ?

HACK-A-TRON, a synergistic event organized by IEEE CAS and IEEE COMSOC,is a unique platform that brings together enthusiasts in the fields of VLSI, Communication Protocols, and IOT - three pivotal domains in Electronics and Communication Engineering.

  • This event offers participants an unparalleled opportunity to not only gain insights from industry experts but also apply their acquired knowledge in a stimulating hackathon.
  • Here, participants' innovative ideas and technical prowess are put to the test, fostering an environment of learning and growth.

The primary objective of HACK-A-TRON is to enlighten participants about the vast array of real-time applications these subjects encompass, thereby highlighting their diversity and relevance in today's technologically advanced world.

This event is designed to inspire, challenge, and empower its participants, paving the way for future innovations in these fields.

About Us

  • 01 About IEEE

    Founded in 1963, IEEE is the largest technical professional organization in the world, with more than 423,000 members from over 160 countries. IEEE is at the forefront of undertaking numerous initiatives worldwide. IEEE is the trusted voice for engineering, computing, and technology information around the globe. It aims to promote science and development in Electrical, Electronics, Computer Science and related disciplines.

  • 02 About IEEE-CAS

    IEEE Circuits and Systems is a students' technical chapter in VIT Vellore with a motto of pioneering solutions to fundamental and applied problems in the fields of circuits and systems. We are a bunch of tech enthusiasts with a great determination in assisting folks here in their journey of acquiring knowledge and awareness about the different technologies and tech advancements around them. We do so by organizing sessions and encouraging projects to make sure our members receive hands-on practical knowledge which can be applied in the real world.

  • 03 About IEEE-Comsoc

    IEEE Comsoc is one of the finest technical chapters of VIT Vellore and the team embraces science, technology, applications and standards for information organization, collection and transfer using electronic, optical and wireless channels and networks. The vision of the Society is to be the organization of choice for communications professionals use throughout the world. The chapter consists of 200+ active members who are extremely talented and result oriented students. We have conducted multiple events in and out of our college and have achieved remarkable success in all the events.

 

FAQ

Unlocking Answers, Easing Queries

  • Duration of the Hackathon ?

    It is a 36 hour hackathon which begins with lectures from top industry experts.

  • What is the judgement procedure ?

    There will be three reviews and the final review will be done by a faculty panel. The winners and the best ideas will be chosen based on the final scores.

  • What is the prize pool for winners?

    Winners will be awarded with cash prize (Amount TBD). Best ideas will also be awarded with cash prize (TBD).

  • What about those who don't win ?

    Win or not, Hack-a-Tron will be learning experience for everyone. However, all participants will definitely get certificates for participation.
